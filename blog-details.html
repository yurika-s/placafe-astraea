The 3rd Anniversary Event

  • Yurika
  • Jul 15, 2024

We are pleased to hold our 3-year celebration event: Distance to Space. Its main activity is a planetarium show in an air dome, which is our new service "portable planetarium" that starts next month. (details for the new service: Starting a Portable Planetarium Service)

You can enjoy the event besides an air dome show such as home planetarium projectors, space goods, and quizzes. Furthermore, we will provide free drink tickets in our cafe for participants following our Instagram account: @placafe_astraea. Do not miss the opportunity.

Event Details

August 14th 12:00 pm to 5:00 pm
Georgian Mall
  • Air dome planetarium show
  • Demonstration of home planetarium projectors
  • Space goods sales
  • Learning and quiz area
  • All of the above for free

** Wheelchairs are available to come in the air dome.

We are looking forward to seeing you there. Thank you for your support always.
