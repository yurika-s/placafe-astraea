Our Story

We started feeling the universe was farther after having come to Canada despite it having great views of the sky and even Aurora. We wondered if that was because of the freezing in winter and the late sunset in summer, and also fewer opportunities to know about the space around us. That's why we opened the cafe to provide a place to feel and learn about the universe in our daily lives. The name "Astraea" came from a goddess in Greek myth. She was the last god to leave human society believing in people until the end and still hopes we will make a peaceful world again. We named the cafe her name with wishes the same as hers.
